HoneyBee Wreaths

Welcome to Honey Bee Wreaths!

One day I was sitting at home bored and I came across the most beautiful wreath I had ever seen on Pinterest. There was a price tag on it that was over $100 and I was floored. I did some reasearch and crunched some numbers. For a simlar size and style I could make it myself for a fraction of the cost. I went right out nad bought all the supplies, posted a picture on Instagram when i was done and the requests started rolling in. Honey Bee Wreaths was born.


Year Round

There is something so beautiful about a classic all year round wreath. It can be something simple like burlap ribbon paired with your favorite flower and a welcome sign. Small touches like this will make it a joy to walk up to your home each night after a long day.
